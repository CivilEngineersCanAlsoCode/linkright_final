# Linkright Viral Content Patterns: Flex Amplification

This profile codifies the high-retention structures and growth loops used by the **Flex Module** to amplify professional career signals.

## 🪝 Viral Hook Library

### 1. The Contrarian Professional

- **Logic**: Challenge a common industry "truth" with evidence from a career signal.
- **Template**: "Everyone says [Common Advice] is the key to [Outcome]. But after [Action in Signal], I realized [Contrarian Truth]."
- **Example**: "Everyone says A/B testing is the key to growth. But after launching Feature X for 500K users, I realized the real bottleneck was user onboarding friction."

### 2. The Transformation (XYZ)

- **Logic**: Use the high-fidelity XYZ formula to show a before/after shift.
- **Template**: "[Old State] was [Pain Point]. I [Action Verb] [X] in [Y] to achieve [Z]. Here is the 1-2-3 of how it happened."
- **Example**: "Product discovery was a black box. I architected a new research loop for our 12-person squad to achieve a 40% reduction in dev rework."

### 3. The "Inside Baseball" (Surgical Detail)

- **Logic**: Provide a granular, technical breakdown of a complex process to build deep authority.
- **Template**: "How to [Complex Task] without [Common Failure]. Step 1: [Granular Detail]. Step 2: [Granular Detail]."
- **Example**: "How to negotiate infra vendor SLAs without losing 40% of your budget. Step 1: Benchmark signal density. Step 2: Leverage cross-org ownership levels."

## 📅 Posting Cadence: The Signal Bridge

- **T+0 (Immediate)**: Share the "Just Shipped" micro-signal.
- **T+48h (Depth)**: Provide a "Behind the Scenes" thread on the trade-offs made.
