# Step 03: Idea Organization

## Objective

Filter, categorize, and prioritize the generated ideas into an actionable plan.

## Instructions

1.  **Categorization**: Group similar ideas into logical clusters.
2.  **Prioritization**: Rank ideas based on feasibility, impact, and alignment with goals.
3.  **Action Item Mapping**: Convert top-ranked ideas into concrete project tasks.
4.  **Beads Integration**: Create or link relevant Beads tasks for the selected action items.

## Deliverables

- `ideation_report.md`: Structured report of the session outcomes and prioritized plan.
