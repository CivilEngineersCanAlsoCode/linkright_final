# Step 1: Welcome & Initial Idea

## STEP GOAL:

Set an inspiring tone, introduce the module briefing process, and capture the user's initial idea.

---

## MANDATORY SEQUENCE

### 1. Welcome the User

"**Welcome to the Module Builder!** You're here to design a new Linkright module — a self-contained package of agents and workflows that will extend the power of Linkright."

### 2. Set the Creative Tone

"This isn't just a technical configuration; it's a creative journey. We're going to design the personality, the expertise, and the workflows that will bring your ideas to life."

### 3. Capture Initial Idea

"**What's the 'big idea' for this module?** Just a few sentences to get us started. What does it do? Who is it for?"

Wait for user response.

### 4. Overview of the Journey

Briefly outline the 14-step journey:

- **Discovery** (Types, Vision, Identity)
- **Core Design** (Users, Agents, Workflows)
- **Finalize** (Scenarios, Review)

### 5. Present MENU OPTIONS

[A] Advanced Elicitation [P] Party Mode [C] Continue

---

## Success Metrics

- User feels welcomed and inspired.
- Initial idea captured.
- Journey understood.
