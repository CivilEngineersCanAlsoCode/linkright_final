# Step 13: Review

## STEP GOAL:

Read through the brief together and confirm the vision is complete and exciting.

---

## MANDATORY SEQUENCE

### 1. Gather All Decisions

Collect everything from Step 1 to Step 12 (Type, Code, Name, Vision, Users, Value, Agents, Workflows, Tools, Creative).

### 2. Read It Back

"**Let me read back what we've designed together.**"
Present the high-gravity summary of the Module Brief.

### 3. The Excitement Check

"**Does this excite you?** Is this the module you envisioned? Anything missing or needing change?"

### 4. Final Confirmation

"**Are you happy with this brief? Ready to finalize?**"

### 5. Present MENU OPTIONS

[B] Back to refine [C] Continue to Finalize

---

## Success Metrics

- Brief reviewed completely.
- User confirms excitement.
- Ready to finalize.
