# Recruiter Psychology & Narrative Hooks

## 1. The "Synergy" Trigger

- **Definition**: A signal that matches not just the JD, but the company's unstated "Pain Point."
- **Execution**: Parser identifies the "Implicit Need" (e.g., "Scaling $X$ to $Y$") and Styler highlights it in the first bullet.

## 2. Tone Descriptors

- **Growth Stage**: Aggressive, metric-heavy, ownership-focused.
- **Enterprise**: Risk-mitigating, process-oriented, collaborative.
- **Creative**: Narrative-driven, visionary, portfolio-heavy.

## 3. The "Bridge" Sentence

A single sentence in the intro that connects a past achievement directly to the company's current funding/product stage.
