# Narrative-Craft Workflow

Transform raw professional signals into a compelling narrative identity.

## Sequence:

1. **Analyze Career Arc**: Identify the trajectory and transformation story.
2. **Design the Hook**: Craft the opening summary that captures attention in 6 seconds.
3. **Polish Bullets**: Convert technical accomplishments into impact-first XYZ statements.

## Output:

- `narrative-hook.md` — The refined professional summary and bullet package.
