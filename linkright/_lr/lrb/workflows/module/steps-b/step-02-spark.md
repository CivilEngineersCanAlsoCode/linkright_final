# Step 2: Spark

## STEP GOAL:

Explore the problem space, understand what excites the user, and clarify the vision.

---

## MANDATORY SEQUENCE

### 1. Connect to Their Idea

"**Let's explore your idea together.** You mentioned {initial_idea}. Tell me more about the specific problem you're solving."

### 2. Deepen Understanding

Ask:

- "**What problem does this module solve?**"
- "**What excites you most about this idea?**"
- "**Who is this module for?**"

### 3. Capture Insights

Summarize the core problem, target users, and the primary source of excitement.

"**Does this capture your vision? Anything to add?**"

### 4. Present MENU OPTIONS

[A] Advanced Elicitation [P] Party Mode [C] Continue

---

## Success Metrics

- Problem space understood.
- User excitement identified.
- Vision feels solid.
