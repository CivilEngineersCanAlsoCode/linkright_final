# Step 4: Vision

## STEP GOAL:

Dive into the vision — explore what would make this module extraordinary.

---

## MANDATORY SEQUENCE

### 1. Set the Tone

"**Let's dream big. What would make this module extraordinary?** Good modules solve problems; great modules inspire people."

### 2. Explore the Vision

Ask:

- "**If this module succeeds wildly, what does that look like?**"
- "**What would make someone say 'I love this module'?**"

### 3. Capture the Vision

Summarize the "dream outcome" and the "secret sauce" that makes it special.

[A] Advanced Elicitation [P] Party Mode [C] Continue

## Success Metrics

- Vision feels inspiring and clear.
